Welcome to the Tsinghua
Wireless Bioelectronics Group

We are an interdisciplinary team that seeks to develop advanced wireless technologies to address important scientific and biomedical challenges in human health. Our current research focuses on wireless communication, sensing, and powering of wearable or implantable devices within living systems. We integrate our wireless technologies in bioelectronic systems to demonstrate innovative approaches to study, diagnose, and treat disease.

Affiliations
